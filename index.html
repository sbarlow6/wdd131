Okomfo Anokye Sword Site – Kumasi’s Legendary Landmark

Kumasi, the capital of the Ashanti Region, is known for its vibrant culture and historical significance. One of its most iconic landmarks is the Okomfo Anokye Sword Site, where the legendary priest Okomfo Anokye is said to have planted a sword in the ground that no one has been able to remove. This site symbolizes unity and spiritual power in the Ashanti Kingdom.
