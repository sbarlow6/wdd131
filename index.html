About Me

Isaac Maldonado, from Santiago, Chile, is a student at Brigham Young University-Idaho, specializing in web and computer programming. Passionate about philosophy, he enjoys studying thinkers like Nietzsche and Aristotle and writing essays, stories, and novels exploring society and morality. His approach combines the logic of programming with the depth of philosophical thought.

Santiago, Chile

Santiago de Chile is the capital and largest city of Chile, located in a valley surrounded by the Andes Mountains. It is the country's political, economic, and cultural center, blending historic and modern architecture. The city offers a vibrant urban life, diverse cuisine, and easy access to nature, with nearby mountains and vineyards.
