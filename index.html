Accra, Ghana

Offical flag of Ghana

Ghana, located along the Gulf of Guinea in West Africa, is a vibrant nation known for its rich history and cultural heritage. Established as the first sub-Saharan country to gain independence from colonial rule on March 6, 1957, it proudly bears the name "Gold Coast" due to its abundant gold resources. The capital, Accra, is a bustling city where tradition meets modernity, home to diverse ethnic groups like the Akan, Ewe, and Ga. Renowned for its lush rainforests, bustling markets, and the iconic Kakum National Park, Ghana also boasts a thriving football culture and warm hospitality.
