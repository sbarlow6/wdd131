Buenos Aires, Argentina

Buenos Aires is the capital city of Argentina, a country known for its rich culture, passionate people, and deep love for football. The citycombines European style architecture with Latin American energy, offering a unique experience full of contrasts. Argentina is famous for its traditional food, especially empanadas, asado (barbecue), the traditional drink, Mate. Music and dance are also an essential part of the culture, especially Tango, which was born in the streets of Buenos Aires. From historic neighborhoods like San Telmo and La Boca to modern areas like Palermo, Argentina invites everyone to explore its charm, warmth, and strong sense of identity.
