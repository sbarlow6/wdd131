Nigeria is a country in West Africa, known as Africa’s most populous nation with over 200 million people. Its capital is Abuja, while Lagos is its largest city and economic hub. Nigeria has over 250 ethnic groups, with the Hausa-Fulani, Yoruba, and Igbo being the largest. It gained independence from Britain in 1960 and has Africa’s largest economy, driven by oil, agriculture, and tech. Nigeria is famous for Nollywood (its film industry), Afrobeats music, and cultural diversity. However, it faces challenges like corruption, infrastructure deficits, and security issues.
