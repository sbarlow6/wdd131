Nature Lens - Site plan

Site Purpose

This site is a porfolio and resource hub for nature photography enthusuasts. it offers tips for beginners, showcases galleries organized by themes (landscapes, wildlife, macro), and provides gear recomendations. The goal is to inspire appreciation for nature through visual storytelling

Scenarios

  • what camera gear is recomended for beginners interested in bird photography?
  • hnere can I see examples of macro photography taken in my local area?

Color Scheme

The chosen color reflect a natural, calming aesthetic suitable for phography-based site focused on the outdoors.

  • Forest Green (#2a5934)
  • Sky Blue (#87ceeb)

Typography

  • Playfair Display - Used for headings to give an elegant, classic feel.
  • Roboto - Used for body text for readability and modern look.
